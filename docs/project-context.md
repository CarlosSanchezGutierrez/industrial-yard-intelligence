# Project Context

## Temporary Project Name

Industrial Yard Intelligence

## Initial Business Case

The first business case is Cooper/T. Smith, an industrial-port operation with yards, docks, bulk materials, general cargo, equipment, infrastructure, and field workflows that may operate without reliable internet access.

Cooper/T. Smith is the initial tenant and validation case. The system must look tailored to Cooper/T. Smith while remaining internally reusable, configurable, and multi-tenant.

## Product Category

Industrial Spatial Operations Platform.

This is not only a GPS app, a map, an ERP, or a WMS.

The product models industrial yards as spatial operating systems where the business can register, visualize, audit, validate, simulate, and analyze operational events.

## Strategic Product Thesis

Industrial yards suffer from poor spatial visibility, fragmented records, manual tracking, weak historical traceability, disconnected field operations, and lack of reliable operational data.

Industrial Yard Intelligence addresses this by turning the yard into a living spatial system made of:

- entities
- geometries
- states
- events
- evidence
- validations
- measurements
- audit logs
- recommendations
- KPIs
- scenarios

## Measurement Vision

The initial system may start with representative maps, simulated orthomosaic layers, manually drawn polygons, manually georeferenced images, and operational-level field data.

The system must be prepared to accept future professional measurement sources:

- mobile GPS
- GPS industrial
- GNSS RTK
- survey pole / bastón topográfico
- drone RTK/PPK
- photogrammetry
- LiDAR
- total station
- GeoJSON
- GeoTIFF
- CAD/GIS
- scales
- external sensors

## Precision Policy

The system must not claim professional precision when the source data is simulated, manually captured, or approximate.

Every measurement must have a confidence level and validation state.

Suggested confidence levels:

- simulated
- approximate
- operational
- georeferenced
- centimeter_ready
- professionally_validated

## Business Goal

The platform must help industrial operators:

- reduce spatial disorder
- reduce operational mistakes
- improve traceability
- reduce time wasted searching or validating material location
- improve visibility of equipment and material flows
- create historical operational data
- support future analytics and optimization
- prepare for professional measurement workflows
