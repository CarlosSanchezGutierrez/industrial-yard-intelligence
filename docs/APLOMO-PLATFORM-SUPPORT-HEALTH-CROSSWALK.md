# Platform Support Health Crosswalk

## Goal

Cross customer health snapshots with internal platform support workflows.

## Route

/aplomo-admin

## What it detects

- Risk customers with no open workflow
- Urgent workflows
- Blocked workflows
- Customers covered by active support
- Healthy customers with no immediate action
- Missing health snapshots

## Exports

- CSV
- JSON

## Why it matters

Aplomo Super Admin now connects risk signals with real operational follow-up.

This is the base for:

- customer success operations
- risk triage
- support accountability
- churn prevention
- expansion workflow
- future AI agents that recommend next actions
